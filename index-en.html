Zeus

is

the origen

and the end;

the beginning

and the outcome;

the start

and the final.

Zeus

is

the power

and the weakness;

the authority

and the freedom;

the action now

and the future;

the loneliness

and the wholeness;

the creation

and the destruction;

the model

and the difference;

the father

and the god;

the life

and the death;

the form

and the limit;

the finitude

and immortality.

Zeus

is

the ancient

and the modern;

the Greek

and the universal;

the orden

and the chaos.

The One.

Zeus

is

and will be

always.

Zeus lives.

José Ángel
